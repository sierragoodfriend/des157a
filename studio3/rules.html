how to play

Players: 2
Goal: Be the first player to score 50+ points

  1. Player 1 rolls the dice to start.
  2. There are two conditions that can harm Player 1:
    • If either die is a one, Player 1 loses all points gained in that round, and it becomes Player 2's turn.
    • If both dice are a one (snake eyes), Player 1 loses their entire accumulated score, and it becomes Player 2's turn.
  3. If neither of these conditions occur, the total of the dice will be added to Player 1's score for that round.
  4. Player 1 can then choose to:
    • Roll again OR
    • Pass their turn, keeping the points gathered so far that round and adding them to their total score
    Repeat steps 1-3 until Player 1 decides to stop rolling or rolls a one.
  5. After Player 1 finishes their turn, Player 2 takes their turn following the same rules.
